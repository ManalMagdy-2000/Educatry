About Us

In the last two years, the COVID pandemic has harmed many children all over the world. One of the priorities is to assist children who need to catch up on "lost learning," particularly those who have not had access to digital resources to keep up with online education. Educatry is a Malaysian charity helps the student to proceed their learning journey by providing digital resources from volunteers and teaching services
